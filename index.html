Ideas for XR Hackathon

Immersive Tourism: AR Time Traveler - City Landmarks

Concept: Create an AR app that allows users to explore famous city landmarks, showing what they looked like in the past using AR overlays.

Key Features:

  • Overlay historical reconstructions on landmarks.
  • Pop-up info panels with historical facts and images.
  • GPS-based triggers to guide users around the city.

Tech Stack: ARCore/ARKit, Unity, Firebase, Google Maps API.

Educational Transformation: AR STEM Learning Assistant

Concept: Build an AR app to help students learn complex STEM topics by visualizing them in 3D. Scan objects or textbooks to bring 3D models to life in AR.

Key Features:

  • Show 3D models of molecules, physics simulations, or geometry.
  • Interactive elements (rotate models, change variables).
  • Optional quiz mode for students to engage with concepts.

Tech Stack: ARCore/ARKit, Unity, Firebase, Blender, Unity Physics Engine.
